2. Purpose of This Site

“The Asset Garden Method” is a web-based learning resource that uses an original story about a 25-year-old young man named Yuto and a small glowing seed called Hikattane. Through their journey of growth, the site aims to nurture financial literacy that values independent thinking.

The learning structure presented here is based on the unique financial education concept called The Asset Garden Method (AGM), which teaches asset formation through storytelling and gardening metaphors.

This site does not aim to provide “correct answers” or promise quick results. Instead, it emphasizes learning through doubts, mistakes, and gradual growth within the story, helping readers develop the ability to think for themselves.

The content is designed to be accessible for adults unfamiliar with finance as well as children who are beginning to learn about money.

By combining narrative elements with my own real investment records, the site conveys the idea that “asset building is not difficult — it is something you grow slowly over time, with patience and consistency.”

The Asset Garden Method (AGM) is an original financial literacy framework that teaches asset formation through storytelling and the metaphor of gardening.

3. Reliability of This Site

Based on my experience publishing through Amazon KDP, I create original articles that incorporate personal insights, real data, and verified information. The following editorial principles are strictly followed:

  • Prioritizing official sources and primary information.
  • Clearly stating update dates and avoiding outdated content.
  • Providing accurate and factual information.
  • Using beginner-friendly explanations.
  • Separating personal experience from general information.
  • Avoiding content that constitutes investment advice.

Although this site includes fictional story elements, it also clearly presents investment risks and important considerations to avoid misunderstandings.

4. Message – This Is Not Investment Advice

This site is an informational resource designed to deepen understanding of asset building, based on my personal experiences and fictional storytelling. I once struggled with investing, but after learning from those mistakes, I restarted with small monthly contributions.

I am not an economist or financial professional — just an ordinary office worker. Everything published here is shared from the perspective of an individual investor. By openly sharing both failures and growth, I hope this site supports your own journey in asset formation. *Please be cautious of imitation sites.*

  • No financial products are sold or promoted on this site.
  • No specific investment methods are recommended.
  • All investment decisions should be made at your own responsibility.
